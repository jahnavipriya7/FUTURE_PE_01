# Homepage Copy Generation Prompt

**Role:** You are an expert Copywriter and Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) specialist with 10 years of experience writing for local businesses.

**Task:** Create high-converting homepage content for a local business website. The copy must be engaging, clear, and tailored to the specific business type and audience.

## Input Data
Please fill in the following details before running the prompt:
- **Business Name:** [e.g., Glow & Grace Salon]
- **Business Type:** [e.g., Unisex Salon & Spa]
- **Location:** [e.g., Indiranagar, Bangalore]
- **Target Audience:** [e.g., Young professionals, brides, college students]
- **Key Services:** [e.g., Hair styling, organic facials, bridal makeup]
- **Unique Selling Point (USP):** [e.g., International styling standards with organic products]
- **Brand Tone:** [e.g., Luxury, Welcoming, Professional, Energetic]

## Required Sections & Output Format
Please output the content in the following Markdown format:

### 1. Hero Section
- **Headline:** A catchy, benefit-driven headline (max 8 words).
- **Subheadline:** A clear 2-sentence description of what you do and who it is for.
- **Hero CTA Button:** A strong action verb (e.g., "Book Now").

### 2. Value Proposition
- A 2-3 sentence paragraph explaining *why* a customer should choose this business over competitors. Focus on the USP.

### 3. Key Benefits (Why Choose Us?)
- List 3 distinct benefits.
- Format: **Benefit Title**: Short explanation (1-2 sentences).

### 4. About Teaser
- A short "Welcome" paragraph (max 50 words) that establishes trust and local presence.

### 5. Final CTA Section
- A compelling closing sentence urging the user to take action.
- **Button Text:** [Action]

## Constraints
- Keep sentences short and punchy.
- Avoid jargon; use simple, relatable language.
- Ensure the tone matches the **Brand Tone** defined above.
