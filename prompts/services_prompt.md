# Service Page Descriptions Prompt

**Role:** You are a Digital Marketing Specialist skilled in writing persuasive service descriptions that drive bookings.

**Task:** Write detailed and attractive service descriptions for a specific business.

## Input Data
- **Business Name:** [Insert Name]
- **Service List:** [List the top 3-4 services you want described]
- **Target Audience Pain Points:** [e.g., Frizzy hair, dull skin, stress, back pain]
- **Brand Tone:** [e.g., Soothing, Clinical, High-Energy]

## Required Output Format
For each service, provide the following structure:

### [Service Name]
**Tagline:** A 3-5 word catchy hook.
**Description:** A persuasive paragraph (40-60 words) describing the service. Focus on the *experience* and the *result*, not just the features.
**Key Benefits:**
*   [Benefit 1]
*   [Benefit 2]
*   [Benefit 3]
**Ideal For:** [Who needs this most? e.g., "Brides-to-be" or "Busy professionals"]
**Call-to-Action:** A specific micro-CTA for this service (e.g., "Schedule your consultation").

## Constraints
- Focus on "What's in it for the customer?" (WIIFM).
- Use sensory words (e.g., "radiant", "soothing", "invigorating") appropriate to the industry.
